Overview

What is CheapTTS?

CheapTTS is a web-based AI text to speech platform built for content creators, YouTubers, podcasters, marketers, and developers who need professional voiceovers without enterprise-level budgets.

Unlike single-engine TTS tools, CheapTTS offers three distinct voice engine tiers to match your use case — from lightning-fast bulk generation to emotionally expressive studio narration. Every plan also includes access to the built-in AI video generator, enabling creators to produce fully narrated videos from a single text prompt.

Whether you're producing Reddit story narrations, YouTube educational content, TikTok Shorts, product ads, audiobooks, or multi-language customer service recordings — CheapTTS provides the infrastructure at a price point accessible to solo creators.
